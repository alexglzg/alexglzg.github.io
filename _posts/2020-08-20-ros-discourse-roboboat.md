VantTec is a group of students of Tec de Monterrey in Mexico that focuses on research and development of autonomous vehicles. The team was founded in 2017 to participate in the RoboBoat competition. Three years later, we won [all the categories](https://roboboat.org/2020/07/26/2020-roboboat-final-standings/) of the RoboBoat 2020 edition.
