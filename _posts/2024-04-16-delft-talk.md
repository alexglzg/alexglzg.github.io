Talk at Delft University of Technology about some of my previous and current research work, and what we do in the MECO Research Team at KU Leuven. I focused on the subjects of robust control and fast optimal motion planning, and their application to single and multi-agent autonomous systems in water, air and ground.

Thanks to Elia Trevisan and Prof. Javier Alonso-Mora for the invitation and tour around the lab, and for facilitating discussions with plenty of researchers working on similar topics.
